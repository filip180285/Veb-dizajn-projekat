About us

The organization was founded in 2021, although its founders have many years of "experience" in the field of protection animal. The activity by which the Association is recognizable is the management of the shelter for abandoned animals "Clifford" in Belgrade. Given that the problem of abandoned animals is still an unresolved issue in our country, our activities are primarily aimed at enabling the survival of shelters. All our animals were chipped, vaccinated and sterilized. Our goal is to find adequate adoption for animals, but how are we not always able to do that let's do it is important for us to provide them with decent conditions and a quality life in the shelter. Our survival and work depend exclusively on donations from citizens and legal entities. As an Association for the Protection of Animals, we are committed to systemic solutions in the field of protection of all animals species, and especially the species that man exploits and uses for various purposes. We are especially active in the field of protection of experimental animals. We stand for the general humanization of society, which implies a radical change of attitude and attitude towards animals. We see the issue of animal rights only as part of the general issue of the right to life.
