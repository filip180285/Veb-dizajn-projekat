      Farms where domestic animals are kept for meat, milk, eggs and other products of animal origin Fur farms, ie domestic and wild animals that are killed for their fur In some countries, there are animal farms from which some other products are obtained, such as farms bears, tigers in China, etc.

Farm animal husbandry is one of the most cruel ways of exploiting animals for profit and for it is related to problems: problems with keeping animals in bad, inhumane conditions, problems related to the killing of animals for meat, fur and other purposes as well as transport problems in the case animals that go to slaughter.

      As an animal rights organization, we are of the opinion that there is no ethical in the modern world justification for this type of animal exploitation. However, organizations that advocate for animal welfare also point out a number of problems related to this species treatment of animals. When it comes to consuming meat, milk and eggs, they emphasize the connection between the violation of well-being animal and food quality while considering the use of fur as non-essential needs is also unacceptable.
